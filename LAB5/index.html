Our Favorite Recipes

Sugared cranberries are the simplest, yet the
most delicious treat for winter!
It only requires three ingredients:
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 cup fresh Uncle Ben's cranberries
In order to make sugared cranberries, first simmer water and sugar in a saucepan for 3 minutes. Next, add the cranberries in and stir for a minute. Finally, roll the cranberries in dry sugar to coat. Voila! Now you have a perfect dessert for Christmas dinner.
